Introduction

Todoist is a powerful task management application trusted by over 30 million people worldwide. It helps you organize, plan, and collaborate on projects of any size, from personal to-do lists to complex team workflows.

Founded in 2007, Todoist has evolved into one of the most popular productivity apps, known for its clean interface, natural language processing, and cross-platform availability. The app makes it easy to capture tasks, set due dates, and track your progress.

Todoist offers a free plan with essential features, while Pro and Business plans unlock advanced functionality like reminders, filters, labels, and team collaboration tools.

Key Features

  • Natural Language Input: Add tasks using everyday language like "Call mom tomorrow at 3pm"
  • Projects & Sub-tasks: Organize tasks into projects with hierarchical sub-tasks
  • Priority Levels: Set priority flags to focus on what matters most
  • Labels & Filters: Create custom labels and filters to organize your workflow
  • Recurring Due Dates: Set tasks to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or custom schedules
  • Collaboration: Share projects, assign tasks, and comment with team members

Download & Install

Official Download: https://todoist.com/downloads

Windows Installation

  1. Visit todoist.com/downloads
  2. Click "Download for Windows"
  3. Run the downloaded installer
  4. Follow the installation prompts
  5. Sign in or create a Todoist account
  6. Start adding and managing your tasks

macOS Installation

  1. Download from the Mac App Store or todoist.com
  2. Open the downloaded .dmg file
  3. Drag Todoist to your Applications folder
  4. Launch and sign in to your account

Linux Installation

sudo snap install todoist

Or using Flatpak:

flatpak install flathub com.todoist.Todoist

How to Use

Adding Your First Task

  1. Click the "Add task" button or press Q
  2. Type your task using natural language (e.g., "Submit report Friday p1")
  3. Press Enter to add the task
  4. Todoist automatically parses dates, priorities, and projects

Organizing with Projects

  1. Click "Add project" in the sidebar
  2. Name your project and choose a color
  3. Add tasks directly to the project
  4. Use sections to group related tasks
  5. Share projects with team members for collaboration

Common Problems & Solutions

Tasks Not Syncing: Ensure you have an active internet connection. Try logging out and back in to force a sync. Check if Todoist servers are operational.
Notifications Not Working: Check your system notification settings. Ensure Todoist has permission to send notifications in your operating system settings.
Natural Language Not Recognized: Make sure you are using supported date formats. Try being more explicit with dates like "Jan 15" instead of "next week."

Conclusion

Todoist is an excellent choice for anyone looking to organize their tasks and boost productivity. Its intuitive interface, powerful natural language processing, and cross-platform availability make it easy to stay on top of your responsibilities.

Whether you are managing personal errands, work projects, or team assignments, Todoist provides the flexibility and features needed to keep everything organized and achieve your goals.
