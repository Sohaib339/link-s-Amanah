Introduction

RescueTime is an automatic time tracking application that runs in the background, monitoring your computer and mobile device usage to give you an accurate picture of how you spend your time. It helps you understand your daily habits and make informed decisions to improve productivity.

Founded in 2008, RescueTime has helped millions of professionals understand where their time goes. The app automatically categorizes time spent on applications and websites, providing detailed reports and insights without requiring manual time entry.

RescueTime offers a free Lite version with basic time tracking, while the Premium plan includes advanced features like distraction blocking, offline time tracking, and detailed reports.

Key Features

  • Automatic Tracking: Runs silently in the background, recording time spent on apps and websites
  • Productivity Scoring: Get a daily productivity score based on your activity categorization
  • Focus Sessions: Block distracting websites and apps to stay focused on important work
  • Detailed Reports: View daily, weekly, and monthly breakdowns of your time usage
  • Goals & Alerts: Set productivity goals and receive alerts when you exceed time limits
  • Integrations: Connect with calendar apps to track meetings and offline activities

Windows Installation

  1. Visit rescuetime.com/download
  2. Click "Download for Windows"
  3. Run the downloaded RescueTime installer
  4. Follow the installation wizard
  5. Sign in or create a RescueTime account
  6. RescueTime will start tracking automatically in the background

macOS Installation

  1. Download from rescuetime.com/download
  2. Open the downloaded .dmg file
  3. Drag RescueTime to your Applications folder
  4. Launch RescueTime and sign in
  5. Grant accessibility permissions when prompted (required for tracking)
  6. Enable screen recording permissions for website tracking in browsers

Linux Installation

  1. Download the .deb or .rpm package from the website
  2. Install using your package manager
  3. Launch RescueTime from your applications menu
  4. Sign in to your account

How to Use

Getting Started

  1. After installation, RescueTime runs automatically in the background
  2. Look for the RescueTime icon in your system tray or menu bar
  3. Click the icon to access quick stats and settings
  4. Let it track your activity for a few days to build meaningful data
  5. Visit the dashboard at rescuetime.com to view detailed reports

Setting Productivity Goals

  1. Log in to your RescueTime dashboard
  2. Go to Goals in the navigation menu
  3. Set a daily productive time goal (e.g., 4 hours of productive work)
  4. Configure alerts to notify you when reaching or missing goals
  5. Track your progress on the dashboard or weekly email reports

Common Problems & Solutions

Not Tracking Activity: Check if RescueTime is running in the system tray. On macOS, ensure accessibility and screen recording permissions are granted in System Preferences.
Incorrect Categorization: You can manually recategorize activities in the dashboard. Go to Activities and click on any item to change its productivity rating.
Data Not Syncing: Check your internet connection. Click the RescueTime icon and select "Sync now" to manually upload data. If issues persist, check firewall settings.

Conclusion

RescueTime is an invaluable tool for anyone looking to understand and improve their productivity. By automatically tracking how you spend time on your devices, it provides the data-driven insights needed to make meaningful changes to your work habits.

Whether you want to reduce time spent on distracting websites, track billable hours, or simply understand where your day goes, RescueTime offers the features and flexibility to help you take control of your time.
